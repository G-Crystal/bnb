CHECK-IN

How do I calculate the guests' arrival time to my apartment?

We recommend you to book your CHECK-IN service 2 hours after your guests' plane landing. We will ask you to provide us their flight number in order to stay updated of their flight status. If your guests will arrive by train, plan an arrival 1 hour later.
Don't forget to recommend a bar or pub near to your property where they can wait in case of an early arrival. Don't worry, our keepers are always extremely punctual, if not early.

My guests are late, what will happen?

Don't worry! Your keeper will wait for your guests up to 45 minutes after the time that you set when you had booked the service. The keeper will be in touch with your guests to confirm their arrival time.

If there is no sign of your guests 45 minutes after the set Check-in time, he/she will contact you to notify you and to make a decision. If their delay had been notified to us with enough time in advance (more than 6 hours prior booking time), we will organize ourselves to postpone the guest's Check-in time. If we are notified with not enough time by either you or your guests, we will be in contact with them to rearrange their reception.

Extra-time after the 45 minutes will be charged $30 per hour (charged every 15 minutes).
Postpone of the service with more than 24 hours’ notice: FREE.
Postpone of the service with more than 6 hours and less than 24 hours’ notice: $10
Postpone of the service with less than 6 hours’ notice: $39.99

I do not know the arrival time of my guests yet, what should I do?

We recommend that you book the service after being notified of the guests' arrival time. We can book your check-in service up to 6 hours before your guests' arrival (subject to keepers’ availability). There is no rush. You can, however, already book for free your key pick-up service, if you are a new client!

How much does a Check-in service cost?

The prices that are offered on the site can vary depending on some factors. To receive the lowest cost, it is recommended that you reserve these services more than 72 hours in advance (between the time of reservation and the date of the service) and the service will be provided between 7am and 7pm.

If the service is required after 7pm or before 7am, an additional NIGHT charge will be applied depending on the time specified.

Likewise, if the reservation is made between 72 and 6 hours in advance, an additional LAST MINUTE charge will be applied. Extra charges apply as well for Sundays and Public holidays. These additional charges are cumulative.

CHECK OUT

How should I program the departure time?

Depending on the departure time preferred by your guests, we recommend that you foresee your check-out service 30 minutes before, in order to have enough time to check up on the state of the apartment, take notes on its state and get the keys back from the guests. It is important to let your guests know that they will have to spend some time with your BNB keeper before being able to leave your apartment.

Do you make a review of the apartment after the check-out?

We will confirm that your apartment has not suffered any damage (torn couch, damaged wall...). Your BNB keeper will proceed then to the secure closing of your apartment:

- closing windows
- turning off lights and heaters
- closing and locking your door
We might not notice a missing fork, but do not fear; if we do notice any problem, we will take pictures and we will contact you immediately to warn you.

CLEANING

What does the housekeeping service include?

Our housekeeping service answers to high quality hygiene standards in order to meet your and your guests' expectations.

It includes:
- Dusting all horizontal surfaces,
- Vacuuming and cleaning of all floors,
- Cleaning of sinks, microwaves and the interior of the fridge in your kitchen, - Cleaning of the shower or bathtub, sink, and toilet,
- Replacing all trash bags,
- Changing of bed linen and bathroom towels.

Regarding the cleaning products used during the services, the keepers will use the ones that will be at their disposal. They will also inform you if some of them are meant to be missing.

In the event of the apartment needing a substantially deeper cleaning, you will be notified by your BNB keeper and will be invited to contact our customer service to agree on a possible additional charge. We recommend you to book a CLEANING PLUS at least once every 2 weeks in order to clean your property more deeply: windows, mirrors, walls, skirting, plants watering, oven, … This option adds an extra hour to the regular time allocated to your keeper.

What is not included in the cleaning service?

The cleaning of exterior parts,
The cleaning of interior and top of cupboards,
The treatment of carpets and mats,
The cleaning of skirting,

If you have specific demands, contact us.

LAUNDRY

How does the laundry service work?

To offer the best service to your guests, you can rent our professional linen! At each booking, our keeper will come with fresh linen and towels!

Between 2 guests, during the cleaning service, the keeper will come with fresh linen and bring back with him/her the dirty one.

That’s easy and don’t have to worry about it.
What is included in the laundry option?

The option is organised by "set of laundry", each set includes 1 fitted sheet, 1 duvet cover, 2 pillow cases, 2 large towels, 1 small towel, and 1 dishcloth.

How much does a LAUNDRY service cost?

The price of the laundry service depends of the length of the rental period. We charge $19.99 per week and per laundry set. This price includes linen rental and washing!

You can select the date and time of your choice for this appointment directly online.
